Positive Emotions In Our Life Beyond Happiness

Wherever we look these days we are surrounded by negative emotions and sadness. Being happy and staying happy takes out a lot of energy from us. People keep saying "be happy" or "stay happy" but what exactly is happiness?

When we say "positive emotions" I bet the first emotion that comes to mind is - happpiness. There is a wide range of emotions that play a part in helping us stay happy. These positive emotions increase our power of resilience and improve the quality of our life.

Feeling positive emotions can also helps improve our resilience power. Positive emotions enhance social skills, empathy, and self -control that helps in bouncing back from stressful events quite easily.
